About the application

The purpose of this application written in MQL4 and python, is to helpo all the person who wants to use the usefull metatrader Expert-Advisor to trade safely on the Binance Platform. By using the Binance Api all the orders you open in MetaTrader will be opened at the same price, with the same StopLoss and TakeProfit, quantity ecc; That's not all, SL and TP will be modified simultaniosely in MetaTrader and Binance and you will be also be able to be able to clos the transaction in Binance when it is closed in MetaTrader or Viceversa. On this page you wil see which one of your orders are opened in MT and which of them on Binance, You will also be able to check the closed Order and get other usefull info!
